Running a small business in India in 2026 is no joke. GST filings, delayed payments, inventory, compliance — it can feel like a full-time job before you&apos;ve even sold anything.

The good news: AI tools finally speak Hindi. Here are the five we recommend every Indian MSME try this year.

## 1. KhataBook AI — Voice bookkeeping in Hindi

Forget spreadsheets. Just speak into WhatsApp: "आज 2000 रुपये का माल बेचा". KhataBook AI records the transaction, calculates GST and updates your dashboard in real time.

**Time saved:** ~4 hours a week.

## 2. LoanSathi — Best loan in 60 seconds

Tell LoanSathi how much you need and why, and it instantly compares loans across 40+ banks and NBFCs. It picks the cheapest combination, lists exactly which documents you need and finds the nearest branch most likely to say yes.

**Use case:** Need a ₹5 lakh business loan for inventory? LoanSathi finds you a 10.5% rate and a 30-minute branch appointment.

## 3. UdyamSathi — Recover delayed payments

If customers delay, UdyamSathi drafts a legal notice under the MSMED Act with interest at 3x the RBI bank rate. It even files your MSEFC dispute if the buyer still doesn&apos;t pay.

**ROI:** Businesses recover ₹1-5 lakh of pending receivables per month on average.

## 4. NiyamMitra — Stay on top of GST and compliance

NiyamMitra tracks every change to GST, TDS and labour laws relevant to your business and sends summaries in plain language. It also generates monthly compliance checklists.

## 5. KisanMitra (if you deal with farmers)

If your business buys from or sells to farmers — FPO manager, input supplier, agri-processor — KisanMitra gives you free access to mandi prices, scheme databases and subsidy calculators.

## The pattern

Notice something? All five tools run on WhatsApp. No new apps. No logins. No training. That&apos;s intentional — Indian businesses already live on WhatsApp, so that&apos;s where the AI has to live too.

## Getting started

Pick the tool that solves your biggest pain and try it free for a week. Most businesses we talk to pay for themselves within the first month.
